Welcome to tweatWELL! tweatWELL is a social network developed for WVU students with the goal of creating behavior change related to fruit and vegetable (Freggie) consumption. It is a unique point-based social network with the purpose of providing a fun way for WVU students to increase their intake of Freggies. Watch the short video clip below to learn more. New to tweatWELL? Signup and get started.

Signing Up

1. Team Sign Up Option:

New to tweatWELL! Sign up as team and compete against your friends to earn the most points and take home a prize. First, select “sign up”. Your username will be your team name, then first initial, and last name. For example, Jane Smith signs up with her friends under the team name Polar Vortex. Her username is PolarVortexJSmith. Teams are made up of 2 or more participants.

2. Individual Sign up:

If you wish to sign up as an individual, use your mix ID when creating your username. You still have the ability to earn a weekly prize based on continued participation with the program.
