Health Scoring:

Your health score is number between 0-100 indicating your level of health based on a few key measurements. 100 is the highest and 0 is the lowest.

Want to improve your health score? It's pretty straight forward. Here are some things simple things you can do to improve your score.

  • Reach and maintain a health body mass index (BMI).
  • Check-in all your activies and exercise.
  • Check-in all your measurements such as blood pressure and weight.
  • Adhere to the health improvement plan established by you and your doctor.
