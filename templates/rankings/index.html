Rankings & Coaches Poll

There are 3 types of rankings. The coaches poll, the point ranking and percent body fat change rankings. As you may have guessed, the coaches poll is determined by the SRC fitness staff and certified personal trainers. Only the top 10 men and top ten women are ranked in the coaches poll. Otherwise you are unranked. Its somewhat subjective...kind of like a college football coaches poll. :-) The coaches poll is updated weekly, while the percent body fat change and the points polls are updated nightly.
