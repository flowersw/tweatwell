FAQ

Q. How do I get started?

A. The first thing you need to do is sign up for the program at http://www.tweatWELL.com. You will need to register with your MIX e-mail address in order to verify you are a WVU student and fill out the registration information. TweatWELL interacts with Twitter in a way where you can tweet your updates, and they will automatically be uploaded to the newsfeed at tweatWELL.com (Please see “How is Twitter helpful with tweatWELL?”). Therefore, it is highly recommended to use your Twitter account or sign up for a Twitter account in order to interact with the website via tweets or texts more easily, although it is not required.

Q. What’s a “Freggie”?

A “Freggie” is short for fruit and veggie.

Q. What are the program dates?

A. January 14 through April 27, 2013

Q. What is the purpose of tweatWELL?

A. TweatWELL is a unique, point-based social network with the purpose of providing a fun way for WVU students to increase their intake of Freggies. This program is designed specifically for students at WVU. The majority of WVU students do not consume the recommended levels of Freggies per day (5-9 servings). WELLWVU believes the more involved you are with this program, the more likely you will be to consume the recommended level of Freggies per day, thus reaping the many nutritional benefits they have to offer.

Q. How is Twitter helpful with tweatWELL?

A. Twitter makes it very easy to post your comments and photos onto www.tweatWELL.com via your twitter account, a cell phone twitter application, or by texting to “40404”. You simply use hashtag “#freggie” when you want to post to www.tweatwell.com, and it will automatically post your comment or picture to the www.tweatwell.com website. Anytime you tweet or text your post, simply use the hashtag “#freggie” and your post will automatically be uploaded to www.tweatwell.com.

For example, “#freggie I ate an orange and one serving of carrots with my lunch today!” or “I ate a banana for a snack and drank 2 bottles of water so far today! #freggie.”

Q. How do I earn points?

A. Points are distributed based on different interactions with the site. The following is a list of how to receive points and the amount of points awarded:

Posting you ate a Freggie 2 points
Earn Professor Badge 1 point
Earn Dean Badge 3 points
Earn President Badge 5 points
Correctly answering the question of the week 5 points
Posting a picture of what you ate 5 points
Spin to Win Variable

There is also a subjective part to the tweatWELL program. WELLWVU will award extra participation points for reaching certain goals, showing improvement, etc. If you noticed you have received more points on the sidebar, it is because we feel like you are on the right track.

Q. How does tweatWELL work?

A. The social network is designed for you to earn points based on the amount of interactions you have with the program. Points are distributed for various interactions among the site, including posting the fruits and veggies you consume, spinning the wheel, etc. (Please see “How do I get points?” for the distribution table). The healthier you eat and the more active you are on the site, the more points you get. It’s that simple!

Q. Is Twitter the only way for me to post comments?

A. No. You can make posts directly at www.tweatWELL.com much like you would use Facebook. However, having a Twitter account will easily allow you to tweet your posts from your phone. Also, by having a Twitter account you can text your posts to “40404” as long as you use the hashtag #freggie.

Q. What is the purpose of the badges on the top right?

A. Each of the four badges represents an accomplishment you have made throughout tweatWELL. They include:

The President badge is normally grayed out, but if it is lit up, you are currently the President of the Freggies! This means that you have consumed the most fruits AND veggies throughout the week and will have a better chance of winning the weekly prize. The President badge will reset at the end of each week.

The Dean badge is normally grayed out, but if it is lit up, you are currently the Dean of veggies OR the Dean of fruits! This means that you have eaten more veggies OR more fruits than any other user this week. Therefore, there will be two Deans each week. You will also have a better chance of winning the weekly prize. The Dean badge will reset at the end of each week.

The Professor badge is normally grayed out, but if it is lit up, you are currently the Professor of a particular fruit or veggie! This means you have eaten more of a particular fruit or veggie than any other user. You will also have a better chance of winning the weekly prize. The Professor badge will reset at the end of each week.

The Joker badge is normally grayed out, but if it is lit up, you have spun the Salad Spinner and landed on Joker this week. This means you have won a coupon for a free piece of fruit or vegetable. If you land on the Joker, we will e-mail you a coupon with more information.

Q. How is a week defined in the tweatWELL program?

A. A new week begins every Monday starting at 12:01 AM. You will be awarded the appropriate badges and maintain that badge for at least a week.

Q. What are the incentives of the program?

Winners: Although the majority of this program is just for fun competition, awards will be given throughout the program. Here’s how:

Each week there will be a raffle. If you have accumulated at least 40 points for the week, you will be entered into the raffle. You can increase your chances of winning the raffle by being the President, Dean or Professor. President will receive an additional 5 names in the raffle, Deans will receive an additional 2 names in the raffle, and Professors will receive an additional 1 name in the raffle.

Points: Points are used as a tool to personally motivate and encourage you to be active on the tweatWELL site and increase your consumption of Freggies each day. Points are awarded for members to use for friendly competition. It is a good representation of how active our members are on the site and how much you are improving your Freggie consumption.

Professor: You can become the “Professor” of a specific fruit or veggie by consuming more of a particular fruit or vegetable than any other user in the tweatWELL program. This score is reset each week and will show the professor of a specific fruit or veggie each week.

For example, the player who is currently consuming the most bananas during the week and posts it on tweatWELL will become the Professor of bananas. You will remain the Professor of bananas until the end of the week.

Dean: You can become the “Dean” of either fruits OR veggies by consuming more than anyone else in the tweatWELL program. This score is reset each week and will show the Dean of fruits and the Dean of veggies each week.

For example, the player who is currently consuming the most veggies and posts it on tweatWELL will become the Dean of the veggies. You will remain the Dean of the veggies until the end of the week.

President: You can become President of all Freggies by consuming the most Freggies when compared to everyone else in the tweatWELL program. This score is reset each week and will show the President of the Freggies each week.

For example, the player who is currently consuming the most Freggies during the week and posts it on tweatWELL will become President of the Freggies. You will stay the President of the Freggies until the end of the week.

Q. How will I benefit from tweatWELL?

A. Hopefully you will:

  1. You can win cool weekly prizes!
  2. Learn how easy it is to eat 5-9 servings of Freggies per day and make lasting changes in your Freggie consumption
  3. Feel the amazing difference in the amount of energy you have when you eat the optimal amount of Freggies every day.
  4. Develop a taste for Freggies that will last for the rest of your life.
  5. Have fun playing with all the features of the tweatWELL program!
